Tian Bai
University of Bergen

Tian Bai | 白 天

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Bergen (UiB), working in the Bergen Algorithms Research Group with Prof. Fedor Fomin. Prior to this, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Hong Kong (HKU), working with Prof. Zhiyi Huang. I received my Ph.D. from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), under the supervision of Prof. Mingyu Xiao. I work on the design and analysis of algorithms, especially exact and parameterized algorithms for graph problems. I also do research on algorithmic game theory.
